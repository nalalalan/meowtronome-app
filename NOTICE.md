# meowtronome media notice

The beat sound uses `assets/meow-cute-clean.ogg` and `assets/meow-cute-clean.mp3`, louder cute-meow derivatives of Wikimedia Commons `Meow.ogg` with the low thump filtered out.
`assets/meow-silas.ogg` is the unchanged source copy, and `assets/meow-silas.mp3` is a browser-compatibility transcode of the same recording.

- Source: https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Meow.ogg
- Description: My cat Silas meowing. by Dan Crosby.
- Author/uploader: Dan Crosby / Dcrosby
- License: Creative Commons Attribution-Share Alike 3.0 Unported
- License URL: https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by-sa/3.0/

The cat dance visual is `assets/cat-dance-cutout.webp`, generated as an eight-pose photorealistic sprite sheet for this app and processed into a transparent cutout.
